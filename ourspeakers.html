Adv Heena Khurana Nagpal

POSH Trainer & Coach

Adv. Heena Khurana Nagpal is a passionate advocate for workplace equality and safety. As a renowned expert and corporate POSH trainer based in New Delhi, she dedicates her career to empowering professionals and educating them on preventing sexual harassment. Through engaging talks and insightful workshops, Heena sheds light on the intricacies of POSH laws, fostering a culture of respect and inclusivity in corporate environments.

Avika Jain

Wunderkind

Avika Jain, a prodigious girl, international speaker, theme dancer, story teller, incredible orator, Guinness World Record Holder, with a mind as sharp as a diamond and an intellect that defies the confines of her age. Avika Jain, with her precocious intellect and insatiable thirst for knowledge, embodies the very essence of genius. She is an active participant in various activities like keynote speaking, dancing, singing, rangoli making and drawing competitions. She has been part of multiple summits alike International Student Exchange Programs, Global Youth Conference, Knowledge Camps, Pet Festivals and Youth Ambassadorships; recognised by PETA.

Dr. Ati Priye

CEO - Incubator - CGC Division of Research & Innovation

Dr. Ati Priye boasts over 14 years of extensive professional experience, serving as the CEO-Incubation at Chandigarh Group of Colleges. Dr. Ati Priye's professional journey encompasses diverse roles in program/project management, Center of Excellence operations, management, and promotion of self-sustainability. He possesses a profound understanding of the MSME/Startup Ecosystem, strategic business within the technology domain, and technology solutions for higher education verticals.
