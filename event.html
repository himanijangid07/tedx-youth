Attendees Rules & Regulations

  • There is no refund for the registration once done. If the registered candidate couldn't attend the event for any reason, refunds for the same will not be done.
  • Only one person may register at a time, and it is not transferable.
  • Registration cancellation and rescheduling are not permitted.
  • Attendee need to certify that the information given is true and accurate by registering and paying for the service.
  • All registrations are subject to verification. If there is a discrepancy, registration may be cancelled by the organizing team without any claim for refunds.
  • Ensure that you have a printout or phone with the ticket information on it for verification at the time of entering the venue.
  • To obtain a conference attendee badge at the venue, each participant must have a valid government identification card with them.

Safety & Security

  • As security checks are conducted, kindly assist the police and private security officers.
  • To protect your safety, the organisers reserve the right to stop and search anyone entering the event.
  • Consuming any illegal substances is strictly prohibited.
  • It is completely forbidden to carry food, drink, cigarettes, firearms, inflammables, and illegal drugs.
  • The organiser disclaims liability for any injury, loss, or theft of the ticket holder's personal property.
  • If there is any type of behavioural infraction, any person may be asked to leave the venue without being responsible for a refund. This choice is final and legally binding and lies with the conference and venue management.
  • Attendees are expected to follow all general venue and attendee guidelines.
